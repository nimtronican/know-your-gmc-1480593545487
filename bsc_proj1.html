Applying NWoM principles to IBM Security Business Partner Communications



Overview:

 Designed a campaign approach to Business Partner communications to enable focus on driving intentional business outcomes through various communications tactics and shift from too many communications with low impact, to the right communication for high impact

Challenge:

    Integration with analytics tools to measure campaign performance and integrating PartnerWorld metrics with Unica/Coremetrics
  • Use of Cohort segmentation to shape content relevance
  • Application of the Buyer Journey approach in the context of Business Partners
  • Application of the integrated Campaign approach to a journey experience
  • Application of the Content Blueprint methodology
Solution:

Encourage Security Business Partners to utilize/deploy the Security campaigns hosted in IBM Digital Content Marketing (DCM) platform and design an analytics tracking framework for tracking all PartnerWorld communication, all based on the New Work of Marketing principles.

Business Results:
    IBM Security communications campaign - Open Badges:
  • Yielded significant uptake (29% increase month to month) in the Security Essentials modules completed and in progress by Business Partners in June
    • IBM Security communications campaign - Enabling BPs for Digital Content Marketing: Campaign usage on IBM Digital Content Marketing (DCM) platform up 50% in June 2016 (monthly usage metrics) IBM Security communications campaign - Driving awareness about Cognitive Security
  • 568 click throughs to Cognitive Security assets/offers in July
